creativity isn’t in my blood, (feat. workflow)

I’ve always loved design, and I still do, but somewhere along the road, I saw that I just wasn’t creative at all, unless it was for someone else’s sake. That’s why I’m a problem solver, not a designer, e.g. when I have had clients I always end up not showing mockups and instead sitting down and giving them recommendations on design and making the changes on the fly as I’m extremely efficient in the adobe apps. But what does that do? Well, it makes the client able to get a grasp of what they want while I guide them and make suggestions on why and how design works. This in turn also makes the client able to clearly express themselves to me in the future about how they want their design to turn out.
